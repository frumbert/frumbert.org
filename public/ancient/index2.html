I make web sites. html

Does your business just need a presence on the web so your clients can find your store? Or do you want to let your clients know the current happenings of your business using newsletters and online communities? Do you want a website that you can edit, but don't know HTML? I build web sites that do these things.

I also develop e-learning content, specialise in supporting both older and newer browsers for your web site content, and developing Moodle sites and plugins.

New sites

I can help you set up new sites, find and register domain names, and create email inboxes for your new site.

Existing sites

If your site is out of date or you don't know how to modernise it, I can help you out.

Technical expertise

I'm a web programmer, but I also diagnose and repair computers and software. You've probably heard the old adage If it's not broken, don't fix it; if it is broken, I'll fix it.
