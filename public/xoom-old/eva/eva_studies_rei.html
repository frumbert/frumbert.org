Shinji Ikari
Thought Process

The mental process of Rei Ayanami is possibly the most difficult to grasp, in that she herself finds it hard to define. She is constantly reassesing herself, defining what exactly she is purely out of what she perceives others want her to be. When we first see Rei, we see a young girl who has been pushed to her limits in tolerance.. and yet she persists, her work is her life; no other definition exists for her, thus she persists.
